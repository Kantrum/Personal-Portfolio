About me

My aspiration is to construct a virtual realm where individuals experience a life devoid of pain or stress, perpetually immersed in happiness. While this may appear idealistic, it has been my dream since my formative years, which is why I pursued a career in software engineering.

I am currently a second-year graduate student at Duke University, specializing in Computer Software Engineering. Proficient in web full-stack development, as well as Android and iOS mobile development, I previously undertook internships at DJI (Shenzhen, CN) in Android development for DJI autonomous vehicles, and at Amazon.com (New York, USA) in backend development for the Amazon Ads API.
